# 📚 SAP BTP Mini Book for Old ABAPers

> *"If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough."* — Richard Feynman

## Welcome

This mini book is designed specifically for **classic ABAP and Fiori developers** who are transitioning to the SAP Business Technology Platform (BTP) world. We use the Feynman technique throughout: no corporate buzzwords, no marketing fluff—just clear explanations with simple analogies, like we're having coffee (or çay ☕) together.

## Who Is This Book For?

- **ABAP developers** with years of on-premise SAP experience
- **Fiori/UI5 developers** familiar with classic deployment
- **SAP consultants** working with multiple clients
- Anyone who's heard "BTP", "RISE", "Joule", "Clean Core" and thought: *"What does this actually mean for me?"*

## What You'll Learn

- 🏗️ **BTP Architecture** — Global accounts, subaccounts, and why they matter
- 🚀 **RISE with SAP** — The managed cloud offering demystified
- 🔗 **Destinations** — The "phone book" for secure connections
- 🤖 **Joule Agents & Skills** — Building AI-powered assistants
- 💼 **Multi-Client Management** — Real consultant patterns
- 🧹 **Clean Core** — What it means for your coding habits

## Philosophy

Every explanation in this book follows these principles:

1. **Start from zero** — No assumed BTP knowledge
2. **Use analogies** — Buildings, apartments, phone books, and neighbors
3. **Show real scenarios** — Consultant life with multiple clients
4. **Be honest** — Acknowledge current limitations and workarounds
5. **Stay practical** — Focus on what you'll actually use
