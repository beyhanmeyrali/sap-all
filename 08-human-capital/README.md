Rachel sits in her cluttered office at 11:30 PM, surrounded by employee files, payroll corrections, and compliance nightmares. As GlobalTech's newest People Operations Director, she's drowning in a tsunami of HR chaos that's about to get worse with the Amazon order scaling requirements:

**The Crisis:**
- Sarah from FI complaining: "Payroll costs don't match our budgets!"

- Clara from PP panicking: "Production scaling needs skilled technicians!"
- Diego from SD celebrating: "Amazon order success - but we need customer service team!"
- CEO declaring: "We're scaling from 500 to 750 employees in 3 months!"

**The Breaking Point Questions:**
- Who's authorized for overtime on the Amazon order rush?
- Which departments are over budget on labor costs?
- How do we track productivity during production scaling?
- Who's eligible for performance bonuses after this success?
- How do we hire, onboard, and train 250 new employees efficiently?

**Rachel's Reality Check:** *"We're managing people like inventory instead of empowering them as strategic assets!"*

**The Revelation:** SAP HCM isn't just employee record-keeping - it's the nervous system that connects human potential with business performance, ensuring the right people are in the right roles doing the right things at the right time.
